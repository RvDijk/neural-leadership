STDP direction (verified against primary literature):
- **Pre-before-post** (Δt > 0, presynaptic spike precedes postsynaptic): synapse **strengthens** (LTP)
- **Post-before-pre** (Δt < 0): synapse **weakens** (LTD)
- Asymmetric time window: roughly ±20-40 ms in hippocampal cultures

**Primary source**: Bi, G., & Poo, M. (1998). Synaptic modifications in cultured hippocampal neurons: dependence on spike timing, synaptic strength, and postsynaptic cell type. *J. Neuroscience*, 18(24), 10464-10472.

**Why:** The story's Act III narrative ("context before ask = strengthen") depends on this direction being right. Confirmed correct in story/index.html as of 2026-05-31.

**How to apply:** Any future scene that touches STDP should preserve this direction. The mapping "upstream fires just before downstream" → strengthen is precise. Caveat: the millisecond timescale of STDP does NOT directly explain quarterly relational learning — that requires systems consolidation / schema learning instead. Soften any text that conflates the two timescales.
