You Are a Weight — Decision intelligence field notes
=================================================

Field notes on applied AI, systems, and decision-making in engineering teams.

About
-----
This repository contains a short, scroll-driven interactive story and a collection of small essays and visualizations exploring how choices, timing, and system structure change the "weights" inside organizations. The content is practical and observational — designed as working notes rather than formal research or product docs.

Goals
-----
- Record concrete examples from applied AI and engineering leadership
- Surface tradeoffs and constraints relevant to real teams
- Offer lightweight, readable artifacts that can be shared on LinkedIn or Slack

View locally
------------
Open `story/index.html` in your browser, or serve the folder with a simple static server:

```bash
python3 -m http.server 8000
# then open http://localhost:8000/story/
```
